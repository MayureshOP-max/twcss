Welcome to Our Palworld Server

Fight, farm, build and work alongside mysterious creatures called "Pals" in this completely new multiplayer, open world survival and crafting game!

Palworld Features

Explore Our Palworld Features

Feature 1

Engage in thrilling hunts: Utilize various tools and strategies to track elusive animals across dynamic ecosystems. From stealthy approaches to adrenaline-fueled pursuits, each hunt presents an opportunity for skillful capture.

Feature 2

Explore vast landscapes: Roam diverse environments spanning mountains, forests, and deserts, encountering an array of creatures to capture and study. Every corner holds unique surprises and challenges to uncover.

Feature 3

Customize your arsenal: Equip yourself with an extensive array of gadgets and gear tailored to your preferred style of capture. Experiment with different traps, baits, and lures to outsmart even the most cunning creatures.

Feature 4

Build a sanctuary: Establish your own sanctuary where captured animals can thrive in natural habitats. Design and manage enclosures, conduct research, and foster conservation efforts to ensure the well-being of your diverse collection.
